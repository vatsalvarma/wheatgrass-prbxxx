Wheatgrass Shot

Shake the blades. Gulp the cleanse.

INGREDIENTS
Fresh Wheatgrass Extract
Water
A touch of Lemon

What’s Special About This Drink?

Wheatgrass is known for its fresh, earthy character and has been valued in natural wellness traditions for its revitalizing qualities. Harvested while the plant is young and nutrient-dense, wheatgrass delivers a concentrated green boost in a small serving.

Shake & Gulp Wheatgrass Shot makes it easy to include this fresh green ritual in your modern routine. Clean, simple, and refreshing, it’s designed to be shaken well before drinking so you get the full goodness in every sip.

Health Benefits

  • Supports natural detox processes
  • Helps refresh and energize the body
  • Provides a concentrated green nutrient boost
  • Supports overall vitality
  • A light, refreshing addition to daily wellness

A small shot that feels like a fresh start.

Who Should Drink This?

Wheatgrass Shot can be enjoyed by everyone as part of a balanced wellness routine.

It is especially helpful for people who experience:

  • Low energy or sluggishness
  • A feeling of heaviness from lifestyle habits
  • A desire to support natural cleansing
  • Nutritional gaps in a busy schedule
  • A need for a fresh, plant-based boost

A simple green reset for modern lifestyles.

Emotional Connect

There’s something grounding about green drinks — a reminder of nature, freshness, and renewal. Wheatgrass has long been part of mindful wellness routines, symbolizing a fresh start and a return to balance.

Shake & Gulp Wheatgrass Shot brings that moment of reset into your day — clean, green, and full of intention.

How to Enjoy

  • Shake well before opening
  • Best taken in the morning on an empty stomach or during energy dips
  • Consume as a quick wellness shot
  • Can be followed with a sip of water if preferred
